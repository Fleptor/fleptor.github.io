Episode 1

Story of DC

Welcome to the 1st episode of Flepness Broadcast, in this episode Fleptor goes over the story of Dark Cloud: how it came to be, Who made it and how it ended.

Now since it's the first time for Fleptor to attempt such a concept it was bound to be flawed, immensely, like he forgets to announce the beginning of chapter two, or the unproperly timed jokes and terribly trying to maintain eye-contact throughout the episode.
But if we set aside technical critiques, we can notice Fleptor's strong passion and that he's genuinely enjoying his performance, which makes it bearable to consume.

As for the script, it generaly stays consistent across the 6 episodes, a self-aware mix of modern standard Arabic and common dialect.
